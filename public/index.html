Mission of the Project

Our mission is to develop an autonomous drone swarm system capable of performing continuous day-and-night patrols over large and complex border areas. Using cooperative UAVs, the system detects unauthorized crossings, tracks moving targets, and provides real-time intelligence to a central monitoring dashboard.

Key Capabilities

1. Drone Coordination

Each UAV communicates with its peers to divide patrol zones efficiently and prevent overlap.

2. Threat Detection

Onboard AI models identify unauthorized movements or suspicious activities in real-time.

3. Target Tracking

The swarm dynamically reassigns drones to persistently follow moving targets across sectors.

4. Live Dashboard

All telemetry and video feeds are streamed securely to a central command interface.
